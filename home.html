ABOUT ME

My name is Belle Lim, and I'm currently a fourth year at Northeastern University studying Computer Science with a focus in human-centered computing and a minor in Experience Design. I am passionate about making elegant, accesible designs come to life that push the bounds of tech for the greater good! Tech is boundless, and so is our ability to use it to help others!
